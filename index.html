Hi, thanks for visiting my page!

My name is Olivia. I am currently a first-year PhD student in Pitt Linguistics.

My research interests focus on sociolinguistics, including:

  • Social and cultural meanings associated with sound
  • Sound change influenced by geographical variation (Mandarin, Cantonese)
  • Intersections of language, gender, and sexuality, particularly online discourse and masculinity

I also have interests in multimodal analysis, corpus-based studies, and language education using technology-driven approaches.
