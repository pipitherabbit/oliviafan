I am currently a first-year PhD student in Pitt Linguistics

My research interests mainly focus on sociolinguistics:

(1) the social and cultural meanings associated with sound

(2) sound change influenced by geographical variation (mainly in Mandarin, Cantonese)

(3) intersections of language, gender, and sexuality (mainly online discourse and masculinity)

I also take additional interests in language education using technology-driven approaches
